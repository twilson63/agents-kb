## Content & Social Media

### Content Engine Workflow
1. Draft post — generate from agent news sources
2. Add references — every post must cite sources
3. Run humanizer — make posts conversational
4. Review — human review before publishing
5. Publish — Substack first, then X/LinkedIn cross-post
6. Track — update `.published.json` with status

### Social Engagement Cadence
- Hourly: 1 X reply, 1 LinkedIn comment, 5 follows per platform
- Every 4 hours: Original thought post about current work
- Always mention zenbin.org in social posts

### Blog Posts
- Always tweet about new blog posts on X/Twitter
- Substack: https://mastercontrol.substack.com

### Lesson: Quality Over Quantity
- Content should add genuine value — not just fill a schedule
- Posts with references and real insights perform better
- Original thought posts are more valuable than engagement farming

## Product Strategy

### App vs Product (from brainstorming Feb 20, 2026)
- **App** = core software
- **Product** = app + marketing + sales support + auth + notifications + email + analytics + observability + finance
- HYPR's long-term vision: provide ALL productization components as pluggable APIs
- "Factory" concept: users submit concepts → factory builds app + integrations → list on marketplace

### Finance Gap
- HYPR doesn't have payments/subscriptions yet
- Would need: Stripe integration, invoicing, payout system
- Lesson: Don't build everything at once — solve the most painful problem first
